Breed Bible Growth Strategy

Complete 5-Phase Plan to Build Traffic and Revenue

1

Content Foundation

IN PROGRESS
  1. Build 30 high-value articles targeting searchable keywords
    • Breed comparisons (Golden vs Lab, GSD vs Malinois, Husky vs Malamute, etc.)
    • Product guides (best food, crates, beds, toys, grooming tools)
    • Training guides (puppy training, behavior issues, commands)
    • Care guides (first year costs, grooming by coat type, senior care)
  2. Article requirements: 2000+ words, proper headings, internal links, Amazon affiliate links with tag
  3. Update sitemap.xml for every new article
  4. Update articles/index.html to list all articles
  5. Update homepage to feature new articles
  6. Verify deployment on live site after each batch
2

Distribution & Community Seeding

PENDING
  1. Reddit engagement
    • Search: "Golden vs Lab?", "best food for [breed]", "first time dog owner help"
    • Write genuinely helpful answers
    • Subtly link to relevant guides when appropriate
  2. Quora answers
    • Find questions about breed comparisons, training, products
    • Answer comprehensively, link to your articles
  3. Dog forums (DogForum.com, The Labrador Forum, etc.)
    • Join conversations, build reputation
    • Share links naturally
  4. Facebook groups
    • Golden Retriever owner groups, Labrador groups, general dog groups
    • Help people, share guides when relevant
3

Link Building

PENDING
  1. Guest post outreach
    • Find pet blogs accepting guest posts
    • Pitch breed-specific articles with links back to your site
  2. Resource page outreach
    • Find "best dog resources" roundups
    • Email site owners to include your guides
  3. Broken link building
    • Find broken links on pet sites
    • Offer your content as replacement
  4. Breed club partnerships
    • Contact breed-specific clubs
    • Offer to write for their newsletters in exchange for links
4

Optimization Loop

PENDING
  1. Monitor Google Search Console
    • See which articles rank
    • Identify opportunities
  2. Double down on winners
    • Expand successful articles
    • Create related content
  3. Update old content
    • Refresh dates
    • Add new products/affiliate links
    • Improve based on search data
  4. Internal linking
    • Connect related articles
    • Pass link equity through site
5

Expansion

PENDING
  1. Email newsletter
    • Capture emails with lead magnet (free puppy checklist)
    • Weekly newsletter with tips + affiliate links
  2. Video content scripts
    • Write scripts for YouTube videos
    • Link back to articles in description
  3. Social media automation
    • Schedule posts sharing articles
    • Auto-post new content
  4. Paid ads (when profitable)
    • Test Facebook/Instagram ads to high-converting articles
    • Scale if ROI positive
