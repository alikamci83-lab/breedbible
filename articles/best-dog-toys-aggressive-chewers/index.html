Toys to Avoid for Aggressive Chewers

  • Cheap vinyl or latex toys: Shred instantly, choking hazard
  • Toys with squeakers: Aggressive chewers will extract and swallow them
  • Stuffed animals: Become stuffing explosions in minutes
  • Rawhide: Can swell in stomach, cause blockages
  • Cooked bones: Splinter and cause internal damage
  • Tennis balls: Abrasive to teeth, easily destroyed

The Rotation Strategy

Even the best toys become boring. Keep 3-4 toys in rotation, swapping them every few days. Old toys become "new" again when they reappear. This extends toy life and keeps your dog mentally engaged.
